In this example, we'll use the Sites API to select a VPS Anchor that you can localize to.

<b>Instructions</b>
Make sure you've been signed in.
Choose an Organization where the site you're looking for is.
Choose the site you'll want to localize to and tap the <i>Localize</i> button.
Physically go to the location you’ve chosen and point the camera towards it.
(optional) follow the arrrow to point you in the general direction of the anchor.
After a few seconds you should see the "Tracking" message and a cube spawn at the wayspot origin.
